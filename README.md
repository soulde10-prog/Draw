## Comprehensive Roofing Contractor Features

### 3D Drawing Capabilities
- Visualization of roofing projects in 3D to enhance client presentations and understanding.

### Roof Types
- **Tile**: Durable and aesthetically pleasing roofing option.
- **Slate**: Elegant and long-lasting, perfect for high-end projects.
- **Corrugated**: Lightweight and economical, ideal for various applications.
- **IBR**: Strong and versatile roofing solution for industrial settings.
- **Flat Torch-on**: Effective waterproofing for flat roofs.

### Roofing Components
- **Valleys**: Essential for directing water to drains.
- **Skylights**: Enhance natural light and energy efficiency.
- **Sidewall Flashings**: Protect joints between the roof and vertical surfaces.
- **Headwall Flashings**: Prevent water ingress at junctions.
- **Undertile Plastic Timber (e.g., 38x38)**: Used for supporting tiles and improving thermal insulation.

### Kingpin Pitch Calculator
- A tool for accurately calculating the pitch of roofs to ensure proper water runoff and aesthetics.

### Square Meter Calculations
- Includes calculations for pitch and valleys to assist in estimating materials required for roofing projects.
